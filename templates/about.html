About

Tom Holohan is a music producer, mix/master engineer and songwriter from Dublin, Ireland. Now based in London, Tom has had the pleasure to work with a wide range of acts in all facets of their projects.


After completing a Bachelors degree in Music Production from Pulse College in Dublin, Tom relocated to London to continue is endeavours into music, working alongside artists in many styles (metal, pop, rock, electronic, hip-hop and more) to create art they can be proud of.
